Cookie Policy

This page explains the cookies and browser storage used when Orelian Ltd operates PFD Toolkit. It covers cookies and similar technologies such as local storage.

Summary

Orelian Ltd currently uses first-party cookies in PFD Toolkit that are necessary for authentication, session handling, security, and one-off messages. The app does not currently load advertising cookies or known third-party analytics tags.

The app also uses browser local storage for interface state, user notices, the product tour, and investigation wizard drafts.

Cookies

Name Set by Purpose Category Typical duration
sessionid Orelian Ltd Keeps you signed in and ties server-side session state to your browser, including login state and saved Explore filters. Strictly necessary Up to 2 weeks by Django default, unless cleared earlier.
csrftoken Orelian Ltd Helps protect forms and authenticated actions against cross-site request forgery. Strictly necessary About 1 year by Django default, unless cleared earlier.
messages Orelian Ltd May be used by Django to carry short success or error messages between page loads. Strictly necessary Short lived; normally cleared after the message is shown.
Auth0 cookies Auth0 Used on Auth0-hosted login/logout pages to authenticate users and protect the sign-in flow. Authentication/security Controlled by Auth0 and the user's browser/session settings.

Browser Storage

Name or pattern Purpose Category Typical duration
pfd_investigation_wizard_draft_v1 Saves an in-progress investigation wizard draft on your device so closing and reopening the wizard does not lose the draft. API keys and CSRF tokens are excluded. Functionality Until the investigation launches successfully or you clear browser storage.
pfd_user_notice_*_seen Remembers that you acknowledged the current site notice version. Functionality Until you clear browser storage or the notice version changes.
pfd_tut_v1_seen Remembers that you have seen or dismissed the product tour. Functionality Until you clear browser storage.

Third-party Analytics and Advertising

We did not find Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, advertising tags, or similar analytics/tracking scripts in the current codebase. If analytics or advertising cookies are added later, this policy should be updated and consent controls should be implemented before they are set.

Managing Cookies and Storage

You can block or delete cookies and local storage in your browser settings. Blocking strictly necessary cookies will prevent login and may break form submissions or other core app features.
