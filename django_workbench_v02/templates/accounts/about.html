Why coroners’ reports deserve more than a filing cabinet.

Every Prevention of Future Death report is a coroner saying, on the record, that someone did not have to die. PFD Toolkit exists to make this archive searchable, analysable, and usable at scale.

The reports

What a PFD is

When a coroner concludes an inquest, they can issue a Regulation 28 report to any organisation they believe could prevent similar deaths.

The problem

Why they go unread

Reports are published as PDFs with minimal metadata. Serious thematic work is otherwise manual and slow.

The approach

AI as an instrument, not an oracle

You ask a plain-English question; the toolkit screens reports, returns matches with supporting quotes, and preserves traceability.

The cost

Why some features need an API key

PFD Toolkit is free/open-source. AI model usage is billed by model providers; users bring their own key.
