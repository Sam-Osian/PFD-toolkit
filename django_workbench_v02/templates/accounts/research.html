PFD Toolkit's foundation evaluation research.

Sam Osian, Arpan Dutta, Sahil Bhandari, Iain E Buchan, Dan W Joyce · University of Liverpool and Mersey Care NHS Foundation Trust

Independent clinical adjudication showed that PFD Toolkit can automate national thematic review of PFD reports with high agreement, high speed, and materially improved case capture.

How it was evaluated

Large corpus, blinded reference standard.

The study screened 4,730 Prevention of Future Death reports (July 2013 to November 2023) and benchmarked Toolkit outputs against a blinded psychiatrist panel on a stratified 146-report sample (73 positive, 73 decoy), with consensus adjudication for disagreements.

Why it matters

Faster surveillance, better signal detection.

The system identified nearly double the child-suicide reports found in the comparator national review (73 vs 37), including reports that pre-dated archive tagging conventions. It also produced structured outputs ready for trend analysis and policy-facing reporting.
